# Direct Outreach — The Shift Series

Goal: earn 10 third-party citations in 30 days from named individuals whose audiences overlap with regulated-industry buyers and senior brand/marketing operators. Citation forms: blog mention, podcast guest spot, newsletter feature, quote-tweet, or syndication.

The pitch is editorial-first. We are not asking for a backlink. We are sending a piece worth reading and naming a specific reason it overlaps with their audience.

## Send rules
- Maximum 3 sends per day. Personalisation per email > 5 minutes or it's not worth sending.
- One follow-up only, after 7 days, if no response. No second follow-up.
- Track in `distribution/outreach-log.md` (TBD): name, date sent, V1/V2, response, citation outcome.
- If a recipient quotes the piece publicly, screenshot + thank publicly within 24h. The thank-you is more visible than the original send.
- Never ask for a backlink in V1. The piece either earns one or it doesn't.
