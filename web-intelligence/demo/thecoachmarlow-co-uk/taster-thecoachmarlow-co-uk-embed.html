But there is no mechanism to capture that interest: no newsletter, no early-access list, no "be first to hear about seasonal menus." A single form above the fold (or on a dedicated landing page) converts one-time curiosity into a bookable audience. For a restaurant at this price point, even 50 captured emails per month — converted at 15% to a future reservation — represents significant incremental revenue per year at full margin, with zero acquisition cost.
