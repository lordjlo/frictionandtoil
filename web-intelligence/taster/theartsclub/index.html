Heritage Brand Hidden Behind a Login Wall

The Arts Club owns one of London's most powerful brand stories — 160 years of cultural patronage, a Mayfair address, and founding members including Charles Dickens — but buries almost all of its cultural programming behind a member portal. The homepage title tag reads "Homepage - The Arts Club," there is no schema markup on any page, and the events calendar is fully gated. This makes the club effectively invisible to AI search engines at the precise moment new rivals are building digital-first presences to compete for the next generation of members. Soho House and Annabel's are capturing non-branded category searches that The Arts Club should own.
