Elite Brand, Zero Digital Sovereignty

5 Hertford Street is arguably the most powerful private members club in London — yet its digital footprint is controlled entirely by third parties. Wikipedia, Spear's, and luxury travel blogs define the narrative; the club's own site returns a 403 to anyone who isn't already inside. In an era of AI-powered search, that means the club has no say in how it's described, recommended, or compared — and rivals with better digital presence are capturing the consideration layer long before a prospective member even hears of 5 Hertford Street (5HS).
