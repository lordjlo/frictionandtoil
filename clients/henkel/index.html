Strategic Moat Assessment
Adhesive Technologies — Specification-In Moat

AT's moat is specification-in at OEM design stage. BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Volkswagen Group, Stellantis, Airbus, Boeing, Safran each carry 12–24 month re-qualification cycles on any supplier switch. Once LOCTITE is embedded in a Fastenal Managed Inventory cabinet (61.3% of Fastenal sales), switching friction rises sharply. LOCTITE holds AI-search category ownership in threadlocking (SKUs 222, 243/242, 263/262) and aerospace structural bonding (EA 9394 C2 Hysol, EA 9696 AERO, EA 9377 AERO). Moat is genuine but not uniform: in EV battery assembly — the fastest-growing application — LOCTITE sits behind DuPont BETAMATE and 3M in AI-search response sets.

Consumer Brands — Narrower and More Fragile

Persil holds 26–28% Germany laundry share — a genuine category lock — but is challenged in UK (11% vs Ariel 25%), under private-label pressure (21.9% own-label volume growth 2021 vs −9.4% branded), in a market contracting at −0.7% CAGR to 2035. OLAPLEX's 180+ patent portfolio runs to 2034 on core BAPD and to 2041 on Bond Shaping Technology (US 12,214,225) — but the category has been colonised by K18 (Unilever), Redken Acidic Bonding Concentrate (L'Oréal), and Wella Ultimate Repair (Coty). Patent exclusivity is no longer the moat. Professional channel trust is.

Governance — Strongest Asset, Currently Mispriced

No hostile approach is possible. No activist can compel separation. This should command a defensive premium but is instead trading at a discount because the market reads the §15 UmwStG Sperrfrist lock-out as permanent trapped value rather than as permanent family-aligned capital underwriting the €5.8B acquisition programme.

Therefore: The AT pure-play narrative — standalone multiple 9–11× vs blended 7.4× — is the single most concrete value-unlock thesis, worth approximately €5–9B of theoretical market value; but it is structurally inaccessible to external pressure until at least H2 2031 and requires operational convergence of the two divisions into pure-play-adjacent profiles over 36–60 months rather than an event-trade.

§15 UmwStG — Legal Framework
§15 Umwandlungssteuergesetz (UmwStG) governs the tax treatment of German corporate demergers (Spaltung/Abspaltung). A tax-neutral demerger requires both transferring and receiving entities to constitute a qualifying Teilbetrieb — an autonomous business unit capable of independent operation — and that neither disposes of the transferred assets within five years of the transaction date. Any disposal triggering the Sperrfrist creates a retroactive taxable event on the entire gain.
Continental–Vitesco precedent: A tax-neutral Abspaltung was executed in Germany at approximately €350M in transaction costs. The mechanism is legally available; the Sperrfrist timing is the binding constraint, not the legal structure itself.
§179 AktG threshold: Structural changes (spin-offs, demergers) require a 75% supermajority at the Hauptversammlung. The Henkel family pool holds 61.85% of ordinary voting shares — insufficient alone. Any structural transaction requires institutional investor coalition support, adding a political constraint on top of the time-locked Sperrfrist table.
